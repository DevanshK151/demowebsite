Colors and fonts

White

White represents purity or innocence. While a bride wearing white was often thought to convey the bride's virginity, blue was once a traditional color worn by brides to symbolize purity. White is bright and can create a sense of space or add highlights.

Dark cyan

Cyan is a relaxing and inspiring color that evokes images of crystal waters and is usually associated with liveliness, tranquility, youth, and energy. It is made of green and blue and represents rationality and cleanliness

Dark red

Dark red is associated with vigor, willpower, rage, anger, leadership, courage, longing, malice, and wrath.
